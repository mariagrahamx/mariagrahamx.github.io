CC3: Debate

Task: Produce two charts that support or refute (or are related to in some way) to a topic of policy debate.

Should the UK implement targeted maternal health programs for ethnic minority women?

The first chart shows maternal mortality is persistently higher among Black, Asian, and Mixed ethnic groups than among White women in the UK.

The second chart supports this pattern: even after adjusting for socioeconomic and gestational differences, babies of Pakistani and Black African mothers face a significantly higher risk of neonatal death compared to babies of White mothers.

Together, these two charts show clear ethnic inequalities in both maternal and neonatal health outcomes in the UK, suggesting that universal services alone may not be sufficient to address these disparities.
